Buttered noodle: the most basic pasta

Buttered noodles

Description

Buttered noodles are a base to so many other dishes. Distiled down to its core you only really need two igredients, excluding water, makes these noodles a quick and tasty meal or side. What really makes this dish special is that the quality and attention you give to the two ingredients will drasticly change the outcome. play with seasonings and toppings and have fun in the kitchen!

Ingredients:

  • 1 box egg noodles
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • Kosher salt
  • Freshly ground pepper

5 Minute Buttered Noodles Method:

  1. Start by bringing a pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add egg noodles and remove from the heat once tender.
  2. Drain the noodles and rinse with tap water before adding the noodles back to the pot. Add 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter, a pinch of kosher salt and freshly ground black pepper.
  3. Mix until the butter has completely melted and the seasoning has mixed evenly across the noodles.
